# 📌 1. What is CRM?

CRM stands for **Customer Relationship Management**.

It is a software system used by companies to:
- Store customer details
- Manage communication
- Track sales activities
- Improve customer service

CRM helps businesses build better relationships with customers.

---

# ☁️ 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

[Salesforce](https://www.salesforce.com/in/?utm_source=chatgpt.com) is a popular cloud-based CRM platform.

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:
- Manage customer data easily
- Track sales and opportunities
- Automate business tasks
- Improve teamwork
- Provide better customer support

### ✅ Benefits of Salesforce
- Easy to use
- Secure cloud storage
- Accessible from anywhere
- Saves time and effort

# 🧩 3. Salesforce Objects

## 🏢 Account
An Account represents a company or organization.

### Example:
- Amazon
- Infosys
- TCS

It stores company details like:
- Company name
- Phone number
- Website

---

## 👤 Contact
A Contact represents a person related to an Account.

### Example:
- HR Manager
- Sales Executive

It stores:
- Name
- Email
- Phone number

---

## 💰 Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or sale.

### Example:
A company planning to buy software products.

It helps track:
- Deal amount
- Sales stage
- Closing date

---

# 🌍 4. Real-World Mapping

| Real World | Salesforce |
|------------|------------|
| Company | Account |
| Employee | Contact |
| Business Deal | Opportunity |

### Example Scenario
A software company wants to sell products to Amazon.

- **Account** → Amazon  
- **Contact** → Amazon HR Manager  
- **Opportunity** → Software Purchase Deal
